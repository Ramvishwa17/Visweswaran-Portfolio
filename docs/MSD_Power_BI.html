MS Dhoni Power BI

The purpose was to visualize key metrics from Dhoni’s career, allowing for an interactive exploration of his achievements and performance trends over time.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

  • Data Transformation:Data cleaning and aggregation in Power BI.
  • Data Visualization: Creating dynamic visuals like bar and line charts, tables, and treemaps.
  • Analysis:Deriving insights from raw sports data, calculating career metrics, and identifying trends.
  • Storytelling with data:Structuring a dashboard to communicate insights effectively and support decision-making.

Data Source

    The dataset was sourced from Kaggle and contains detailed statistics of MS Dhoni's ODI matches, including runs scored, strike rate, average, and performance against different opponents and at various venues.

Data Preparation and Transformation

1. Cleaning and Importing Data

The raw CSV file was imported into Power BI, where data cleaning steps were applied. Key tasks included:

  • Addressing missing values.
  • Formatting certain columns for analysis.

2. Calculated Columns and Measures

Calculations were created to derive key metrics, including:

  • Total Runs Scored
  • Batting Average
  • Hundreds and Fifties Count
  • Total Sixes
  • Strike Rate

3. Aggregations and Grouping

Data was aggregated to provide insights at various levels, including:

  • Year-by-year performance.
  • Performance against each opponent.
  • Performance at different grounds.

Dashboard Design and Visualizations

The Power BI dashboard consists of the following sections:

  • Key Metrics Overview: A section at the top displays Dhoni’s career summary, including total runs, average, hundreds, fifties, sixes, and strike rate. These metrics provide an instant snapshot of his overall performance.
  • Performance Over Time: A combined bar and line chart visualizes runs scored per year alongside batting average, highlighting trends and fluctuations in performance across his career.
  • Top 10 Scores: A treemap showcases Dhoni’s highest individual scores, which helps in identifying his most impactful innings.
  • Performance Against Opponents: A table shows detailed statistics against each team, including matches played, total runs scored, balls faced, strike rate, and average. This allows for a comparative view of his performance against different opponents.
  • Ground-wise Performance: Another table details runs scored at various cricket grounds, helping to analyze Dhoni's performance in different venues.

Insights and Analysis

The Power BI dashboard consists of the following sections:

  • Trend Analysis:The year-by-year performance chart revealed periods of peak performance and fluctuations, showing consistency and changes in form over the years.
  • High-impact Innings: The Top 10 Scores visualization highlighted his ability to play high-scoring innings in critical moments.
  • Opposition and Venue Analysis:By breaking down performance against various opponents and at different grounds, insights were gained on teams and venues where Dhoni excelled or struggled.

Conclusion

This Power BI report provides a comprehensive view of MS Dhoni’s ODI career. The interactive visuals allow users to explore specific aspects of his performance and gain insights into his playing style, consistency, and impact on the field. This project not only demonstrates the use of Power BI for data visualization but also highlights the analytical approach to understanding sports data.
